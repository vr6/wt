    Helping Ourselves

    Data on this page is made possible through the valuable contributions from several cool folks who are helping in fetching data from USCIS by running a script to pull status for the cases. This data is stitched together and visualized on the front page.

    Some great folks made their mark in data updates. The 2017 updates were ruled by a girl band lead by math (mathangi) with 15k updates and sneha with 7k updates. The 2018 updates are being lead by konstantin, tauseef and several others with their relentless contribution. I can't thank these folks enough!! Kudos to you all!

    If you want to make suggestions or contributions to imrpove this site, please send me an email, or post your thoughts on the comments page.
